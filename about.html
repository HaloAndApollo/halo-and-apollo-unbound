About

My name is Josh. This is Halo and Apollo.

Welcome to Halo & Apollo Unbound — a travel blog about life on the road across America in an RV.

Halo is the dog. A Belgian Malinois — high drive, food obsessed, toy obsessed, always ready to go. She's a people dog, not a dog's dog. Put her in front of a stranger and she's happy. Put her next to another dog and she'd rather not.

Apollo is the cat. Shy, cautious, but endlessly curious. He spends most of his time watching the world go by through the window. He and Halo have an agreement — they play fight, they tolerate each other, and somehow it works.

Together we're figuring out what it means to trade four walls for the open road.

We left Virginia on I-40 West. The plan is to make our way out to Oregon, then start a slow zig-zag up and down the country — no rush, no fixed end date. Just whatever's out there.

We're doing this because we didn't want to wait. Didn't want to save the good stuff for retirement. The hikes, the views, the weird little towns — Halo and Apollo deserve to see all of it. So do I.

This blog is where it gets written down.

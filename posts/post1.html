The Beginning

Every journey has a first mile. This is ours.

Halo — a dog who thinks every rest stop is the greatest place on earth. Apollo — a cat who has strong opinions about everything and trusts no one. And me, somewhere in between, trying to hold it all together while crossing a country I've barely seen.

We packed what fit, left what didn't, and pointed the RV toward the horizon. This blog is where I'll write it all down — the good miles and the bad ones, the weird towns and the beautiful ones, the moments that make you wonder why you ever lived any other way.
